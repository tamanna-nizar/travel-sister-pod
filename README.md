### Project Description
Travel Sister Pod Matcher is a smart and safe platform for women travelers that finds compatible companions based on destination, budget, travel style, and interests. It forms small travel pods and generates personalized day-wise itineraries, ensuring a fun, safe, and worry-free trip.

### The Problem Statement
Women travelers often face safety concerns and difficulties in finding compatible travel companions, which can make solo trips stressful or less enjoyable. There is a need for a solution that helps them form small, safe, and compatible travel pods while planning personalized itineraries.

### The Solution
Travel Sister Pod Matcher forms small, safe, and compatible travel pods for women travelers by matching users based on destination, budget, travel style, and interests. It also generates personalized day-wise itineraries, making trips fun, organized, and worry-free
